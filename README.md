# Financial-Complaints-Analysis
📊 Financial Complaints Analysis Dashboard
1️⃣ Project Title

Financial Complaints Analysis – Business Intelligence Dashboard

2️⃣ Short Description

This project presents an end-to-end financial customer complaints analysis using Power BI and Python. The dashboard analyzes complaint trends across products, issues, submission channels, and time periods to identify key drivers of customer dissatisfaction and potential areas of operational improvement.

The objective is to transform raw complaint data into actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making and improve customer experience.

3️⃣ Tech Stack

Python – Data cleaning, preprocessing, and exploratory data analysis

Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard development

Data Modeling – Relationship building, calculated measures (DAX), KPIs, and YoY metrics

Statistical Analysis – Trend analysis and performance comparison

4️⃣ Data Source

Dataset: Maven Analytics – Customer Complaints Analysis Dataset

The dataset contains financial complaint records including:

Complaint type

Product category

Submission channel

State

Resolution status

Monetary relief

Date of complaint

5️⃣ Features & Highlights
🔎 Business Problem

Financial institutions receive thousands of customer complaints annually. Without structured analysis, it becomes difficult to:

Identify high-risk products

Understand seasonal complaint trends

Track resolution efficiency

Optimize complaint handling channels

Improve customer satisfaction

The organization requires a centralized dashboard to monitor complaint patterns and identify operational improvement areas.

🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

Monitor total complaints and year-over-year growth

Identify products generating the highest complaints

Analyze complaints by issue and sub-issue

Track complaint submission channels

Evaluate monetary relief trends

Enable state and year-level filtering for targeted analysis

📈 Business Insights & Impact

Deposit accounts contribute the highest complaint volume, indicating potential service or process inefficiencies.

Significant YoY growth in complaints signals increasing customer dissatisfaction or increased customer base.

Transaction-related issues dominate sub-issue categories, suggesting need for process optimization.

Complaint peaks during mid-year months indicate possible seasonal operational strain.

Monitoring complaint channels helps optimize digital vs traditional support strategies.
